About Us

You Are Not Alone
in This Journey

I'm Sampath, a counselling psychologist and family therapist, and I run MindCare Plus in Trivandrum. This space is an extension of the work we do every day — supporting people through emotional struggles, relationship concerns, and neurodevelopmental challenges at any age.

At MindCare Plus, we believe that every individual has the potential to lead a fulfilling and meaningful life. We strive to empower our clients to overcome challenges and achieve their goals through a collaborative, strengths-based approach.

Our goal is simple: to offer trustworthy support, practical help, and a safe space to move forward — whatever your need may be. Welcome. You're in caring hands.

S
Sampath Panicker Counselling Psychologist & Family Therapist 📍 Nanthancodu, Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala
